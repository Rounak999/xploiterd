About me

Hi, I’m Rounak Dhadiwal, a Senior Security Engineer with over six years of experience in application security, penetration testing, source code review and product security. I specialize in web and API security, cloud security audits on AWS, and threat modeling, helping organizations strengthen their products from design to deployment.

Along the way, I’ve reported high-impact vulnerabilities to the leading bug bounty programs and Government of India, while also creating and delivering CTF challenges, trainings, and workshops to upskill teams and foster security awareness.
